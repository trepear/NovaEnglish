Why Choose us?

What distinguishes Camp Nova from other camps? At our camp, children encounter English throughout the day. We do not divide the day into an educational part and a leisure part. Our campers are in a team of English and Czech speakers from waking up until the evening. Native speakers are part of all activities. Our camp leaders are experienced teachers with a lot of experience. Leaders are selected based on good references and our experience. Of course, there is a clean extract from the criminal record and a medical certificate. We have been cooperating with more than 80% of our employees for a long time.

Campers come to Camp Nova, because it's not just an English camp, we are much more. Thanks to all the active ones on offer, it is also a place where they can experience new things and deepen their knowledge of nature, sports and gain self-confidence. 

 The camp program is designed as an active rest with English language instruction. A number of sports activities await children, during which they constantly use English (naturally and without feeling afraid and worried about failure). However, the program also includes such activities as: camp skills, tying knots, making fires and cooking in nature, the basics of first aid, etc. Thanks to the beautiful environment of the Jeseníky Mountains, children can try out the orientation in nature, including reading a map and work with compass. 

A children's summer camp with English is a great way to overcome nervousness and develop communication skills in the language.
